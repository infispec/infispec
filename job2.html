Node.js Intern

Full-Time

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Server-Side Development: Collaborate with our development team to build and maintain server-side applications using Node.js. Contribute to the development of efficient and scalable solutions.
  2. Documentation: Play a pivotal role in our technical documentation efforts by documenting code changes, best practices, and usage guidelines for Node.js projects. Ensure that our documentation remains comprehensive, accurate, and up to date.
  3. Code-Documentation Alignment: Bridge the gap between development and documentation by actively participating in code reviews and discussions. Translate intricate technical concepts into clear, user-friendly documentation.
  4. Knowledge Sharing: Foster knowledge sharing within the team by creating tutorials, guides, and documentation resources. Assist fellow developers and technical writers in understanding Node.js concepts and implementation.
  5. Continuous Learning: Stay updated on Node.js and server-side JavaScript development trends, tools, and best practices. Leverage this knowledge to improve your skills and contribute to the excellence of our projects.

Qualifications:

  • Strong foundational knowledge of JavaScript and server-side development concepts.
  • Eagerness to learn Node.js and server-side JavaScript development.
  • Exceptional communication and writing skills.
  • Keen attention to detail and a commitment to producing high-quality documentation.
  • A collaborative mindset and the ability to work effectively within a cross-functional team.

Experience

Worked on atleast 2 Node.js projects
