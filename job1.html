React.js Intern

Full-Time

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Front-End Development: Work alongside our development team to create and optimize user interfaces using React.js. This hands-on experience will enable you to develop responsive and interactive web applications.
  2. Documentation Assistance: Play a crucial role in supporting our technical writers and developers by documenting code changes, best practices, and guidelines for React.js projects. Your contributions will ensure that our documentation remains comprehensive and up-to-date.
  3. Code Collaboration: Actively participate in code reviews and discussions, bridging the gap between development and documentation. This will involve translating complex technical concepts into clear and accessible documentation.
  4. Learning and Knowledge Sharing: Embrace a culture of continuous learning by staying updated on React.js and front-end development trends, tools, and best practices. Share your newfound knowledge with the team, contributing to our collective growth.
  5. Project Support: Provide valuable support to our development projects, assisting with tasks such as bug fixes, feature enhancements, and application testing.

Qualifications:

  1. Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  2. Enthusiasm for learning React.js and front-end development.
  3. Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  4. Attention to detail and a commitment to producing high-quality documentation.
  5. A collaborative mindset and the ability to work effectively within a cross-functional team.

Experience

Worked on atleast 2 react.js projects
